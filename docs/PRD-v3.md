## 1. Product Overview

### What is Aviniti?
Aviniti is an AI & App Development company based in Amman, Jordan. We help SMBs (Small and Medium Businesses) digitally transform by building custom AI-powered applications and offering ready-made solution accelerators.

**Tagline:** "Your Ideas, Our Reality"

### Why This Website?
This website serves as our primary lead generation and conversion tool. It must:
- Showcase our AI expertise and portfolio of live apps
- Convert visitors into qualified leads through interactive AI tools
- Build trust and credibility with potential clients
- Support both English and Arabic-speaking markets
- **Capture abandoning visitors with intelligent exit intent**
- **Provide 24/7 lead qualification via AI chatbot**
- **Demonstrate ROI to justify investment**

### Target Audience
- **Primary:** SMBs going digital (50-500 employees)
- **Decision Makers:** CTOs, Founders, Product Managers
- **Markets:** MENA region + Global English-speaking
- **Budget Range:** $8,000 - $50,000+ projects

### 3.13 Exit Intent System *(NEW FEATURE)*

**Purpose:** Capture visitors who are about to leave without converting. Last chance to provide value and capture lead information.

#### Trigger Conditions:
- Mouse moves toward browser close/back button (desktop)
- Tab switching behavior (desktop)
- Scroll to top quickly after extended page time (mobile proxy)
- Back button press (mobile)
- NOT triggered if:
  - User has already converted (submitted a form)
  - User has dismissed popup in this session
  - User is on legal pages (Privacy, Terms)

#### Exit Intent Variations:

**Variation A: Lead Magnet Offer**
- **Trigger:** First-time visitors, non-specific pages
- **Headline:** "Wait! Before You Go..."
- **Subheadline:** "Get our free guide: '10 AI App Ideas That Are Changing Industries'"
- **Visual:** eBook cover mockup
- **Form:** Email only
- **CTA:** "Send Me the Guide"
- **Secondary:** "No thanks, I'll pass"

**Variation B: Consultation Offer**
- **Trigger:** Visitors on services/solutions pages
- **Headline:** "Have Questions?"
- **Subheadline:** "Book a free 15-minute consultation with our team"
- **Visual:** Calendar icon or team photo
- **CTA:** "Book Free Consultation" (→ Calendly)
- **Secondary:** "Maybe Later"

**Variation C: Quick Estimate**
- **Trigger:** Visitors who viewed pricing/estimate content
- **Headline:** "Get Your Estimate in 60 Seconds"
- **Subheadline:** "Our AI can give you a ballpark figure right now"
- **Form:** 
  - Project type (dropdown)
  - Email
- **CTA:** "Get Quick Estimate"
- **Secondary:** "I need more time"

**Variation D: WhatsApp Connect** *(NEW)*
- **Trigger:** Mobile visitors or MENA region IPs
- **Headline:** "Prefer WhatsApp?"
- **Subheadline:** "Chat with us directly - we respond within minutes"
- **Visual:** WhatsApp icon
- **CTA:** "Open WhatsApp" (deep link)
- **Secondary:** "Send Email Instead"

**Variation E: Chatbot Activation**
- **Trigger:** Visitors who haven't interacted with Vanity
- **Headline:** "Need Help Finding What You're Looking For?"
- **Subheadline:** "Our AI assistant can help guide you"
- **CTA:** "Chat with Vanity" (opens chatbot)
- **Secondary:** "No thanks"

#### Design Specifications:
- Modal overlay with dark background (70% opacity)
- Centered card design
- Close button (X) in top-right
- Click outside to dismiss
- Smooth fade-in animation
- Mobile: Slide up from bottom

#### Behavior Rules:
- Only show once per session
- Don't show if user has converted
- Track which variation was shown and result
- A/B testing capability between variations

## 5. Interactions & Animations

### General Principles
- Smooth, purposeful animations (not gratuitous)
- Performance-first (no animation jank)
- Respect reduced-motion preferences

### Specific Interactions

**Scroll Animations:**
- Elements fade up and into view as user scrolls
- Staggered animations for lists/grids
- Parallax effects on hero section (subtle)

**Hover Effects:**
- Cards: Slight lift (y-axis) + shadow increase
- Buttons: Color shift + subtle scale
- Links: Underline animation

**Loading States:**
- Custom branded loading spinners
- Skeleton loaders for content
- Progress indicators for multi-step forms

**Page Transitions:**
- Smooth fade between pages
- Maintain scroll position where appropriate

**Counter Animations:**
- Numbers count up when scrolled into view
- Easing that slows at the end

**Hero Animation:**
- Device mockups with subtle floating motion
- Code snippets that appear/animate
- App screens that rotate or transition

**Chatbot Animations:** *(NEW)*
- Smooth expand/collapse
- Typing indicator dots
- Message slide-in from bottom
- Subtle attention pulse on first load

**Exit Intent Animation:** *(NEW)*
- Fade in background overlay
- Modal slides up or scales in
- Smooth dismiss animation
